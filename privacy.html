Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

JustCallTech collects phone numbers for authentication via OTP. We also collect name, address, and location data provided during profile setup to facilitate doorstep service delivery.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide IT repair and computer services. We use your phone number for login verification and your address to send technicians to your location.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information.

4. Location Data

We collect location data only when you book a service, to help our technicians navigate to your address. Location data is not shared with third parties.

5. Third Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage, and Google Maps for navigation. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required to provide our services (e.g., sharing your address with the assigned technician).
